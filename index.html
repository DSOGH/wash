Instructions

1

Make the "Sour Milk"

Combine milk and vinegar in a cup. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until it looks curdled. Do not skip this—the acid creates the fluff!

2

Whisk Dry Mix

In a large bowl, whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Ensure there are no lumps.

3

Combine Wet & Dry

Whisk eggs and vanilla in a separate bowl, then add the sour milk and melted butter. Pour this into the dry ingredients.

4

The Gentle Fold (Secret Step)

Use a spatula to gently fold just until combined. STOP when you still see white flour streaks. Lumps are good! Over-mixing = rubbery pancakes.

5

Rest & Cook

Let batter sit for 5 minutes. Heat a buttered pan over medium heat. Pour batter, then drop chocolate chips on top by hand. Flip when bubbles appear (2-3 mins). Cook until golden.

💡 Chef's Note

If the pancakes are browning too fast before fluffing up, lower the heat slightly. Leftovers freeze perfectly—just pop them in the toaster!
